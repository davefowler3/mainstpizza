Only the freshest ingredients

Main Street Pizza has been making preparing Italian and American cuisine for over thirty years! We use only the freshest ingredients sourced from local farmers, dairies and gardens. We know that you can get a pizza anywhere, so we go out of our way to ensure that the one you get from us will be like no other you've ever had. From starters to dessert, we've got all of the specials and classics that you've come to know and love.

We now have two locations to make take out and dine-in even easier! The same menu is available at both locations. We are still open for dine-in, just with limited customers allowed. Have a big event coming up? NO PROBLEM! We do catering too! Stop in one of our locations or call us to schedule great food for your next event!
